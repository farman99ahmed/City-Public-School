Facilities

City Public School provides students with list of facilities that becomes an important tool in their process of learning. We support the ideas and the zestful temperament of young minds of our school's family in every aspect possible.

Technology

We provide the latest computers with loaded features and all time active internet connection to keep the children in touch with the developing technology. A well-equipped computer lab is more resourceful than a library in this era of artificial intelligence.

Library

We value the priority of our children and entertain their choice of learning mode. Well-equipped and up-to-date library with over 3000 books of various genres is a resource for ever expanding curiosity of students in the school. Any student can access the library under the guidance of the school's librarian and use it for their purposes.

Smart Classes

Video Based lectures and use of animation is a part of our teaching program. We believe in the idea of "different minds have different ways of learning". We support and help students out in their time of need to understand the concepts of their syllabus by integrated use of latest technology and traditional explanations.

Conveyance

An all direction transportation system of City Public School helps students and guardians to skip the problem of conveyance. We provide bus and other vehicles for students living in 25 kilometres radius of the school. All our vehicles are tracked by the latest GPS present and school keeps the record of punctuality of our vehicles and eventually of the students.
