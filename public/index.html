About Us

City Public School was established in year 2011 with an aim to fulfil the basic need of a promising education institution. To provide the upcoming generation with world class opportunities and a platform for every developing mind to enhance into a fantastic one are our priorities. Affiliated to CBSE New Delhi we provide the best in class education, convenient and accessible to all the strata of society.

Vision and Mission

Vision

City Public School on its path to achieve the targets, is driven by a vision. Our vision is to prepare our children for life by broadening their horizons so that their world encompasses the whole of mankind.

We want the young minds to deepen their thoughts so that their learning becomes the means to achieve that perfection of mind in which analytical reasoning goes hand in hand with logical conclusion.

Helping our children to incorporate the essential values of goodness, honesty, humility and discipline as an integral part of their being is an integral part of our vision.

We have in mind the way our students need to be guided to become contented individuals whose strength lies in their ability to face adversities without fear and overcome challenges with confidence.


Mission

Our mission is to nurture our students to realize their own potential and identify and achieve their goals in an environment of rich tutelage, joyful and harmonious learning while keeping alive the spirit of togetherness and service before self.

Our aim is to bring forth responsible citizens of the world who make a difference and who will make the school and nation proud of their achievements and stellar personal qualities.
